Chipmunk

Overview: A social search mobile app that helps Iowa State students meet spontaneously on­campus based on activities. You can think of it as a crossover of Meetup and Tinder, but with a focus on small group size on meetings between unknown college students.Technologies include geolocation, matching, and peer-to-peer system. The app is first thought of as a way to help international students get social around campus and connect with Americans.

Role: Product Designer and Head of UX

Pactio

Overview: Pactio was a journalism tech startup based out of Stanford University. How Pactio works in practice is a mix of the crowdfunding strategies deployed by journalism funding experiments, such as the now-defunct Spot.Us and Beacon Reader, and the focus on committed, paying readers shouldering one-person shops like Toast founder Daniel Mallory Ortberg’s Shatner Chatner or the many independents on Patreon.
